# FAQ

## Is PIXN an AI model?
No. PIXN does not implement or ship any AI models.

## Is PIXN decentralized?
No. Decentralization is not implemented at this stage.

## Why no token?
Because there is no economic loop yet. Introducing a token now would be premature.

## What is the goal of the current stage?
To validate execution boundaries and architecture with runnable code.

## Who is this for?
Developers, architects, and technical investors evaluating early infrastructure concepts.
